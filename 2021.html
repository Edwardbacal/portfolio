BIO

edward d bacal makes, does, and consumes things

his professional work centres on communications and media, with an emphasis on digital content and design

his academic work addresses the intersections of aesthetics and theory in 20-21st century art & visual culture

his non-professional work includes music, illustration, and making picture books for his niece

he also likes bikes, bracecephalic dogs, audiobooks, good sound, progressive ideas, travel, cafes, and more

RESUME

edward d bacal is a multi-talented, PhD-educated professional, with an inclination for creative vision and critical thinking. His skillset spans writing, design, content strategy, and web development, offering a holistic approach to digital and print media.

WRITING

edward d bacal writes mostly on modern & contemporary art / visual culture from north america, latin america, and europe. he is particularly interested in abstract and conceptual art since the 1960s, with emphasis on work that explores the political implications of formal innovations.
