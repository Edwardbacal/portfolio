• BUHI TRAX

  • BUHI trax is a long forgotten (but very real) record label that was dedicated to releasing music by a wide variety of unknown (also very real) artists.

    From its first release in 1996, B-T acted as a network for unconnected producers in and around toronto, a platform for isolated artists to release their work into the world.

    Today, the BUHI TRAX discography archives a unique moment and feeling in electronic music, offering a glimpse of a time and place when the dream of a future that would never come was still possible.

  • In 1996 a group of DJs and producers in Toronto, sharing a sense of alienation from the established electronic music scene in their city, combined their efforts and resources to do something on their own. .
