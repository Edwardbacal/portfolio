ABOUT THIS PROJECT

LET'S IRON OUT A FEW WRINKLES was made by Edward D Bacal from August-September of 2025

Edward D Bacal selected the tracks, sourced from records he owns and has an existing relationship to.

Edward D Bacal made the mix, and edited several of the tracks, in Audacity.

Edward D Bacal designed and hand-coded the website and made the illustrations and title-animation.

Edward D Bacal wrote the blurbs.

This project is the digital version of a project (still in progress) intended for cassette tape. If you are interested in receiving a cassette version, please contact Edward D Bacal.
