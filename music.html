• BUHI TRAX

  • BUHI trax is a long forgotten and very real record label dedicated to releasing music by a wide variety of unknown, also very real, artists.

    From its first release in 1996, B-T acted as a network for unconnected producers in and around toronto, a platform for isolated artists to release their work into the world.

    Today, it archives a unique moment and feeling in electronic music, offering a glimpse into a time and place when the dream of a future that would never come was still possible.
